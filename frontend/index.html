Process

From idea to video
in four steps

No editing skills. No software. Just type and watch the magic happen.

1

Describe your topic

Type anything — a subject, a question, a story. The more specific, the better the result.

2

AI writes the script

Groq's Llama 3 generates a full narration script tailored to your chosen style and duration.

3

Visuals + Voice assembled

Relevant video clips and images are fetched and synced to a natural AI voiceover.

4

Download your video

Get a fully rendered MP4 with subtitles and background music — ready to share anywhere.
